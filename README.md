📥 Instagram Following Scraper (No Login Required)
🔍 What is Instagram Following Scraper?

Instagram Following Scraper automates the process of collecting public following list from Instagram profiles—without requiring login, session ID, or cookies.
Just provide one or more Instagram usernames, and the scraper will fetch their following in bulk with support for pagination, scheduling, and easy exports.

The scraper retrieves:

Following username

Full name (if available)

Profile picture URL

Account privacy status (public/private)

Profile verification status

All extracted data is strictly limited to publicly available information. Nothing private or sensitive is collected.

💡 Why Scrape Instagram Following?

Public following data can unlock powerful insights for research and growth:

🔎 Audience Analysis – Understand the following base of brands, creators, or competitors.

📊 Market Research – Identify shared audiences, niches, and engagement patterns.

🧠 Competitor Insights – Analyze competitor reach and discover overlapping audiences.

🤝 Influencer Marketing – Collect potential leads from influencer following.

📈 Growth Strategy – Spot collaboration opportunities and replicate successful tactics.

⚖️ Is it Legal to Scrape Personal Data?
